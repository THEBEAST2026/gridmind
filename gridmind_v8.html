05 — Quantum Heuristic

Quantum-Inspired
Action Filtering

When ρ > 0.85 on any line, a correlation matrix identifies statistically "entangled" lines. The PPO agent focuses exclusively on these — cutting action search space during critical faults.

Line Correlation Matrix 20×20
Step 01
Detect overload
Monitor ρ across all 20 lines. Any line exceeding 0.85 triggers the quantum filter immediately.
Step 02
Compute entanglement
Query pre-computed correlation matrix. Lines with r > 0.7 correlation to the overloaded line are flagged "entangled."
Step 03
Prune & prioritise
PPO action space is pruned to entangled lines only — dramatically reducing search space during critical moments.
